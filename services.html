Garden Maintenance Services in Leyton

I provide a garden maintenance service for all domestic gardens in the E10, E17 and E20 areas. I can help with:

  • Maintenance of existing gardens (including caring for existing plans and beds)
  • Planting of new beds or gardens (including helping with plant choices and sourcing)
  • Lawn care (in the Spring and Autumn)
  • Clearing weeds ready for planting
  • Providing horticultural advice - I can help you understand how to care for your garden by creating a personalised maintenance plan for your garden
