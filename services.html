Garden Maintenance Services in Leyton

I provide a garden maintenance service for domestic gardens. I work primarily in Waltham Forest, but will consider projects in nearby areas. I am a trained horticulturalist with a strong understanding of plants, soil, and garden design. I can help with:

  • Maintenance of existing gardens, including caring for existing plans and beds.
  • Planting of new beds or gardens, including helping with plant choices and sourcing.
  • Lawn care, especially treatments to keep grass healthy in the Spring and Autumn.
  • Clearing weeds to get beds and larger areas ready for planting.
  • Providing horticultural advice and guidance to help you understand how to care for your garden.

If you want help with your garden but don't know where to start, I can offer a free consultation.
